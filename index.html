Who we are

Lucky Shrub is a thriving medium-sized garden design firm nestled in the heart of Tucson, Arizona. Specializing in comprehensive garden design and creation, maintenance, and landscaping, Lucky Shrub has established itself as a one-stop destination for clients looking to transform any space into a verdant oasis to be proud of. In addition to its design services, the company operates a small but thriving plant nursery, offering a curated selection of both indoor and outdoor plants.

Our founders

Lucky Shrub was founded by the dynamic husband-and-wife team, Jason and Maria. Their shared passion for plants and landscaping inspired the creation of the company. Jason, the resident garden architect, channels his creativity and expertise into crafting unique and enchanting designs. He not only conceptualizes the vision but also oversees the implementation through his dedicated team of landscapers. Maria, the marketing maven of the duo, takes charge of all promotional activities for the company and manages the day-to-day operations of the nursery.

What we offer

Garden Design and Creation: Lucky Shrub specializes in crafting personalized and aesthetically pleasing garden designs tailored to meet the unique preferences of each client.

Maintenance: The company offers comprehensive maintenance services to ensure that clients' gardens continue to thrive long after the initial design and creation.

Landscaping: Lucky Shrub's skilled landscaping team brings Jason's designs to life, transforming outdoor spaces into captivating landscapes.

Plant Nursery: With a diverse selection of indoor and outdoor plants, the nursery serves as a hub for plant enthusiasts seeking to enhance their living spaces.

Philosophy

At Lucky Shrub, the philosophy revolves around a deep appreciation for nature and a commitment to creating spaces that not only meet the client's expectations but exceed them. The founders believe in the transformative power of well-designed green spaces and aim to make this experience accessible to everyone.
